About BROWSE 2025

BROWSE 2025 is a premier national level technical symposium organized by Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumakuru. It brings together the brightest minds in technology to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and explore cutting-edge innovations.

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of tech leaders and provide a platform for students to demonstrate their technical prowess through a variety of challenging and exciting events.

Participating Branches

Computer Science Engineering

Exploring the fundamentals of computing, algorithms, programming, and software engineering to build the digital world.

Focusing on information processing, data management, networking, and secure information systems.

Master of Computer Applications

AI & Data Science

Exploring data collection, analysis, and applying artificial intelligence to extract insights and patterns from complex datasets.

Specializing in artificial intelligence algorithms, machine learning models, and intelligent systems development.

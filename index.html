About BROWSE 2025

BROWSE 2025 is a premier national level technical symposium organized by Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumakuru. It brings together the brightest minds in technology to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and explore cutting-edge innovations.

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of tech leaders and provide a platform for students to demonstrate their technical prowess through a variety of challenging and exciting events.

UI/UX PRINT

  • Teams can use AI tools only as specified by the club. No other tools are allowed.
  • A maximum of 10 prompts per AI tool is allowed.
  • Teams must call volunteers every time a prompt is made.
  • Participants must log into their own accounts and ensure AI tool history is saved.
  • VS Code is mandatory for development.
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are compulsory; other frameworks may also be used.
  • Teams must share their GitHub report at the end of the build.
  • 3 members per team.

HYPERTHON

  • Topics will be given on the spot.
  • No prework is allowed — development starts only after topic reveal.
  • Participants will have 3 hours to build the prototype.
  • Any and every AI tool is allowed during development.
  • A working prototype is mandatory for evaluation.
  • Individual participation only — no teams.
  • Participants must be able to explain the methodology and tech stack used.

PITCHATHON

  • ● 3 members per team.
  • ● Round 1: Present core idea via PPT.
  • ● Round 2: Submit a persuasive video pitch (for shortlisted teams).
  • ● Round 3: Solve a surprise challenge under constraints.
  • ● Only PPT can be prepared in advance; all other work must be done during the event.
  • ● All submissions must be original.
  • ● Decisions are final; clarity, innovation, and feasibility are key.
  • ● Adhere to deadlines and maintain professional teamwork throughout.
